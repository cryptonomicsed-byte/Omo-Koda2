# ADR 4: Odu Canonical Identity

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Identity in Ọmọ Kọ́dà is culturally and cryptographically grounded.

## Decision
The 256 Odu Ifá system is the canonical model for agent identity (soul). While BIPỌ̀N39 may be used for mnemonic and wordlist infrastructure, it must not replace the Odu soul model. Every agent's personality traits and behavioral DNA are derived from their Odu seed.

## Consequences
- Ensures a unique and culturally significant identity for every agent.
- Provides a stable, deterministic foundation for behavioral variation.
- Distinguishes Ọmọ Kọ́dà from generic AI agent systems.
