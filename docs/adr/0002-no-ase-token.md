# ADR 2: No Àṣẹ Token

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Previous iterations of the project (e.g., Swibe) introduced a complex three-token economy including an Àṣẹ token.

## Decision
There will be no Àṣẹ token in Ọmọ Kọ́dà. Human-facing payments will be conducted in SUI only. Dopamine and Synapse will be used strictly as internal, metabolic resources.

## Consequences
- Simplifies the economic model.
- Avoids the complexities of cross-token conversion and burn mechanics for users.
- Aligns with the "sovereign by design" goal by keeping internal resources purely metabolic.
